About Sanskruti Makeover

At Sanskruti Makeover, we believe beauty is an art — and we bring it to life with passion and precision. Our expert stylists and beauticians offer personalized care using only the finest products and techniques to enhance your natural glow.

From bridal makeovers to skincare, every service is designed to pamper you. Visit us in Thane and experience the transformation you deserve.

Meet the Founder

Mrs. Vanita Chaudhary is the heart and soul of Sanskruti Makeover. With over a decade of experience in beauty and bridal styling, she has helped hundreds of clients discover their most confident selves.

Her passion for empowering women through beauty shines in every service we provide. You're not just getting a makeover — you're becoming part of a family.
